Welcome to the Auction Simulator

Objective:

  • Each player starts with $50 million.
  • Your mission is to make accurate price estimations for the artworks and strategically win as many as possible while managing your budget.

For each artwork, players will be presented with an opportunity to guess the starting bid.

  • If your guess is correct, the estimated bid will be added to your budget.
  • There is no penalty for an incorrect estimation. You do not lose any money.

Players can purchase additional clues to help refine their estimate. Each clue costs $10,000 and can provide valuable insight into the value of the artwork.

After viewing clues (optional), players can guess the final price for the artwork.

  • If the actual price is higher than your estimate: You will not win the artwork, but your budget remains unaffected.
  • If the actual price is lower than your estimate: You win the artwork, but the estimated price you guessed will be deducted from your budget.
